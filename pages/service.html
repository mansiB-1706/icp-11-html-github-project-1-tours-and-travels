1. Book Tickets for Flight



1. Access a booking platform: Use an airline website or travel agency site to initiate the booking process.
2. Enter travel details: Specify departure and arrival cities, travel dates.
3. Choose a flight: Compare options based on price, duration, and airline preference.
4. Passenger information: Provide full names and contact details.
5. Payment and confirmation: Complete the payment to finalize the booking and receive an electronic ticket.
2. Book Tickets for Railway



1. Online booking: Most train tickets are booked online through the IRCTC platform.
2.Details required: Departure and arrival stations, travel date, class, number of passengers, berth preference.
3. E-ticket: Once booked, you receive an electronic ticket which can be accessed digitally.
4.Physical booking: Option to book tickets at a railway station counter.
